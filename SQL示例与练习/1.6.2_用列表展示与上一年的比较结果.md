# 1.6.2 用列表展示与上一年的比较结果

## 问题:
**准备数据:**

**问题:**
比较每一年与上一年的销量, 并输出如下结果:
```textmate
 year | sale | var
------+------+-----
 1990 |   50 | ▬
 1991 |   51 | ↑
 1992 |   52 | ↑
 1993 |   52 | →
 1994 |   50 | ↓
 1995 |   50 | →
 1996 |   49 | ↓
 1997 |   55 | ↑
```

## 解法:

### 解法一(使用关联子查询):
```sql
select s1.year,
    s1.sale,
    case when s1.sale = (select sale from sales s2 where s2.year+1 = s1.year) then '→'
        when s1.sale > (select sale from sales s2 where s2.year+1 = s1.year) then '↑'
        when s1.sale < (select sale from sales s2 where s2.year+1 = s1.year) then '↓'
    else '▬' end var
    from sales s1;
/*
 year | sale | var
------+------+-----
 1990 |   50 | ▬
 1991 |   51 | ↑
 1992 |   52 | ↑
 1993 |   52 | →
 1994 |   50 | ↓
 1995 |   50 | →
 1996 |   49 | ↓
 1997 |   55 | ↑
*/
```
### 解法二(使用外连接):
```sql
select s1.year, s1.sale,
    case when s1.sale = s2.sale then '→'
         when s1.sale > s2.sale then '↑'
        when s1.sale < s2.sale then '↓'
    else '▬' end var
    from sales s1 left outer join sales s2
        on s1.year = s2.year + 1;
/*
 year | sale | var
------+------+-----
 1990 |   50 | ▬
 1991 |   51 | ↑
 1992 |   52 | ↑
 1993 |   52 | →
 1994 |   50 | ↓
 1995 |   50 | →
 1996 |   49 | ↓
 1997 |   55 | ↑
*/
```
